This document synthesizes findings for online marketplaces selling Palestinian products, categorized for integration into the **Palestine Resilience Field** master inventory.

## 3. General & Platform-Based

- **Etsy**: Numerous individual Palestinian-owned shops (Search: "Made in Palestine").
- **Shop Palestine**: MECA (Middle East Children's Alliance) store, supporting humanitarian aid.

## Strategic Integration for Field Node
These vendors are candidates for:
1. **Drop-shipping integration**: Automating order flow via Shopify/Etsy connectors.
2. **Master Inventory Population**: Extracting product metadata (SKU, price, description) for the Articulation Node.
3. **BDS Compliance**: Each vendor listed here focuses on fair trade and supporting local Palestinian producers.
