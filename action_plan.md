# PALESTINIAN MARKET REALITY TEST - 1-PAGE ACTION PLAN

## WEEK 1: CONTACT & SAMPLES
**Goal:** Establish contact with 2 Palestinian businesses

**Actions:**
1. Email Canaan Fair Trade: info@canaanpalestine.com
   - Request product catalog
   - Ask for sample options
   - Inquire about minimum order

2. Email Zaytoun CIC: info@zaytoun.org
   - Same questions as above
   - Note response time and helpfulness

3. Order 2 product samples ($50-100 total)
   - Canaan olive oil (500ml)
   - Palestinian dates (250g)

**Success Metrics:**
- 2 businesses contacted
- 1-2 responses received
- Samples ordered

## WEEK 2: RECEIVE & EVALUATE
**Goal:** Physically receive and evaluate products

**Actions:**
1. Track shipping time
2. Document customs process (if any)
3. Evaluate product quality upon arrival
4. Take photos for marketing

**Deliverables:**
- Physical products in hand
- Shipping timeline documented
- Quality assessment notes

## WEEK 3: MARKET TEST
**Goal:** Test market interest

**Actions:**
1. Show samples to 5 friends/family
2. Ask: "Would you buy this? At what price?"
3. Post product photos on social media
4. Note any interest or inquiries

**Deliverables:**
- 5+ pieces of customer feedback
- Social media engagement metrics
- Initial interest assessment

## WEEK 4: DECISION
**Goal:** Make go/no-go decision

**Questions to Answer:**
1. Are products good quality?
2. Is supply chain reliable?
3. Is there market interest?
4. Are margins sufficient?

**Decision Criteria:**
- ✅ Proceed if: Positive on 3+ questions
- ⚠️  Modify if: Positive on 2 questions
- ❌ Stop if: Positive on 0-1 questions

## TOTAL INVESTMENT
- Time: 4 weeks, 2-3 hours/week
- Money: $100-150 for samples/shipping
- Risk: Minimal (learning investment)

## NEXT STEPS IF PROCEEDING
1. Place small commercial order ($500-1000)
2. Test sell through pop-up or online
3. Scale based on results
